Top 10 most beautiful beaches in the East

This summer you don't have to go too far to the Maldives or Hawaii, in the east, the countries here also have beautiful paradise islands like dreams known as "raw gems" due to retaining the pristine beauty of people. Now, let's explore the Top 10 beautiful eastbound beaches below to schedule an incredibly exciting summer.


1. Banul Beach, Philippines



Banul Beach is a beautiful place in the Philippines. White sand here is washed by crystal clear aquamarine waters, from which dark sheer cliffs “grow”


2. Nacpan Beach, Philippines



Nacpan Beach is considered the best not only in El Nido, but throughout the Palawan. This is a very picturesque and deserted, but at the same time well-equipped beach, which seems endless.


3. Yanui Beach, Thailand



Yanui Beach is located between the Windmill observation deck and the famous Promthep Cape in the very south of Phuket. Yanui is widely known among tourists, especially fans of snorkeling, diving and kayaking.


4. Beach Phra Nang, Railay, Thailand



Phra Nang Beach is located at the southern tip of Railay, a peninsula located in the Andaman Sea. Phra Nang Beach is famous for its white sand stretches along with cliffs blocking the two ends of the beach. Especially here, food and drinks are sold right on the boats along the beach. You can choose a beer, lie in the sun and watch people try their hand at mountaineering.


5. Tanjung Aan Beach, Indonesia



Tanjung Aan Beach is a snow-white beach with bright blue waters, dense forests and high mountains. It is ideal for swimming, sunbathing, diving and surfing. On its territory there are many bars, cafes and restaurants.


6, Beach Otres, Sihanoukville, Campuchia



Otres Beach is known for many small fishing villages located close to the sea, smooth white sand in the shape of a semicircular length up to 3km long and clear blue water. Otres' villages and temples are also only about 1km from the beach so if you want to learn more about the lives of the indigenous people, you can go there by route from Ochheuteal across the hill is to arrive. The life of the people here is mainly based on fishing and trading tourism services although small but the quality of service is quite good. In addition, the nightlife at this beach is extremely bustling with trendy restaurant bars. This is the perfect choice if you want to have a busy summer.


7. Lazy Beach, Campuchia



Lazy Beach is located on the coast of Koh Rong Samloem. The trip to the island by boat is 2 hours, but it's worth it.


8. Teluk Nipah, Pangkor, Malaysia



Teluk Nipah Beach is located on the western side of Pangkor Island, opposite the Strait of Malacca. Snow-white sandy beach with crystal clear blue water is a famous gathering place for lovers of snorkeling, diving, water skiing and picnics. You can get from the mainland to this beach by ferry in 45 minutes.


9. Tanjung Rhu, Langkawi, Malaysia



Tanjung Rhu impresses with its long, white and extremely clean beach. Unlike the beauty of the Maldives-style beaches, the sea in front of Tanjung Rhu is not endlessly empty but adorned with islands and mountain ranges. The sea water here is not turquoise, as colorful as the Maldives but the waves are very small, a great place for anyone who wants to soak in the cool blue water to relax. If you come here when the tide recedes, you can admire a long sand whip running straight to the remote islands.


10. Las Cabanas Beach, Philippines



Las Cabanas is one of the popular beaches located in the vicinity of El Nido. There is not only a beautiful coastline, but also good infrastructure, so this section of the coast is crowded.
