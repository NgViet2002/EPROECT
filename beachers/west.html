Top 10 most beautiful beaches in the West

This summer you do not need to go too far to the Maldives or Hawaii, in the west, the countries in this direction also have beautiful paradise islands like dreams dubbed "raw gems" due to retaining the pristine beauty of man. Now, let's explore the Top 10 beautiful westbound beaches below to schedule an incredibly enjoyable summer.


1. Ipanema Beach, Brazil



Many people know this beach not from geography lessons and not from their own travel experience, but thanks to bossa nova's famous jazz song "The Girl From Ipanema" ("Garota de Ipanema"). Its authors, Antonio Carlos Jobim and Vinicius de Moraes, made this coast equally famous as Copacabana. Interestingly, in the translation from Tupi, this compound word means "bad, dangerous waters". In fact, the waves in this area are quite aggressive, so the beach is definitely a place worth visiting for surf lovers, but beware of those who come here just to swim.


2. Cabo San Juan Beach, Colombia



El Cabo San Juan beach is located in Tayrona National Park on the Caribbean coast in northwest Colombia, 32 km from the city of Santa Marta. El Cabo San Juan beach is a beach formed among lagoons and tropical jungle. Today it is one of the 20 most beautiful beaches in the world. El Cabo San Juan beach is known for its stunning location, white sand, clear water of emerald color. This beach is popular for camping, which is located on the beach year-round.


3.Akumal Beach, Mexico



Akumal Beach is famous for snorkeling with turtles, in translation the name of the beach means "place of turtles", who have lived on this beach for many years. Stingrays are also frequent guests on the coast. And pelicans often walk around the beach, who are not afraid of tourists at all , expecting that they will treat them with something.


4. Playa Del Amor Beach, Mexico



Hidden Beach, Playa del Amor, Love Beach - these are all the names of one famous beach in Mexico with the romantic name Love Beach, which is located in a hinged cave. As the Mexicans themselves admit, this place is not at all a miracle of nature, but the result of a test of military weapons and equipment.


5. West bay Beach, Honduras



The cozy beach West Bay with white sand and slender palm trees is located in the western part of Roatan Island.


6, Santa Teresa Beach, Costa Rica



The Huge sandy beach of Santa Teresa lies on the shore of the village of Mal Pais which merges with the city of Santa Teresa in the South-Western part of the Nicoya Peninsula. Nearby, there is the great national Park Santa Teresa with the richest on the Pacific coast terrestrial and underwater flora and fauna.


7. Monterrico Beach, Guatemala



Monterrico Beach on the Pacific coast of Guatemala is located on the territory of the resort of the same name in Santa Rosa Department.


8. Johnny Cay Beach, Colombia



Johnny Cay beach is located on a small island in the Caribbean, which is also called the Sugar Island due to the white sand beaches. It is located just 15 minutes by boat from the large island of San Andres. Johnny Cay Beach is a tropical paradise. The island on which it is located is famous for its coral bottom and bright fish, so many people choose this beach for snorkeling.


9. Beach Tulum, Mexico



It is located in a distance from the city of Tulum, about six kilometers. The beach is covered with fine sand, with minor waves in calm and sunny weather. Strong winds reach the coastline at the end of the season - in April, and rains before the start of the high period - in September.


10. Barra da Lagoa Beach, Brazil



Barra is one of the best beaches in the city of Inhambane, Mozambique, the Indian Ocean coastline. Geographically it is located between the center and the port of Beira.
