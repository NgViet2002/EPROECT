Chesterman beach

Chesterman is a free municipal beach in British Columbia, located in western part of Vancouver, in the village of Tofino.


Beach description

The beach is sandy, the coastline is long and wide and stretches to Frank Island. This is a quiet place popular with Canadians from different cities of the country. Tourist season lasts from July to September, the rest of the time the resort doesn't fit for a beach holiday. It's a great place where you can relax and have fun. The south of the beach is windless – the coastline is protected by rocky mountains and growing dwarf trees. This is used by tourists who want to sunbathe, swim peacefully, athletes who swim in kayaks and kayaks. In this area, the shore is gentle, there aren't many reefs and underwater rocks at the bottom. The water is clean and calm in the south part.


During summer people sunbathe on the beach, have picnics, play beach volleyball, frisbee, sail on kayaks, canoes and go surfing. In winter the place is popular with people who like to watch professional surfers and the ocean during the storm. During the low tide on the coast, the beach becomes wider, there is a lot of wet sand to create sculptures, castles. In 2016, the resort was recognized as one of the most beautiful places for a vacation in the world ranking. Accommodation available at beach houses, hotels, apartments.

When is it better to go?

In most of Canada, there is a mild, temperate climate, in the north is subarctic. The average temperature in July is +21 degrees in the south and up to -4 in the Arctic archipelago. Winters are cold – in the south +4 degrees, in the north to -35. Precipitation is up to 1250 mm per year in the north and up to 2500 on the coast of the Atlantic and the Pacific Ocean. A sharp frequent change in atmospheric pressure. It is not hot in the country. On the Atlantic coast, the weather is unstable - it is warm in summer, storms in winter, snowfalls. The best time to relax in Canada is the summer months.

Infrastructure

The beach in Livadia is distinguished by good infrastructure, which provides visitors with comfortable vacation. Following facilities are available:

equiped car parking;


rental of beach equipment - umbrellas, deck chairs, sale of rubber rings;


cafe where you can have a meal during the day;


souvenir stores and shops;


toilets and changing rooms.


Lovers of wild outdoor recreation can set a tent right on the beach and live in it for several days. For this purpose there is zone for camping tent equiped on the territory of the beach. Amateurs of a more comfortable vacation have the opportunity to stay at a nearby hotel or at one of 90 bases located near the beach area.

Attractions operating during the summer period - trampolines, slides, water rides and other entertainments - will help diversify a beach holiday not only for children, but also for adults. In a peak season you can not only swim and sunbathe, but also ride bananas, catamarans, ATVs, parachute after a boat.
