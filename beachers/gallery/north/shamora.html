Shamora Beach

Shamora is the largest, cleanest and most popular beach near Vladivostok. The name is given in the 70s, Lazurnaya Bay, was not widely spread among the residents of the Primorsky Region. By many indicators, the local beach is among the top ten beaches in Russia. The unique landscape of the Ussuri Bay attracts vacationers not only from the Far East.


Beach description

The lush vegetation of the hills and rocks of the outstanding capes in the sea separates the sandy shores with the purest water from all the fuss. The beach is isolated from the road, always cleaned, equipped with attractions, entertainment complexes. There are cafes and bars. You can stay in nearby summer houses, tent camps, boarding houses, recreation centres.


The coast and bottom of the bay are sandy, the water is warm in summer, completely safe. Clinging jellyfish are not observed here. Rocks and algae are found in some places. It takes a long time to go deep, which adults do not like very much, but the kids - the very thing. You can lie on soft sand on your own towel, take a bench, a gazebo, take other beach or sports equipment at the rental shop.

The territory of the beach is wide, takes about 2 km in length. It is conditionally divided into 3 parts due to the streams flowing here. The zone at the central entrance is considered the most civilized, restaurants work here, there is a market, a supermarket, in the evening the embankment is lit. The second beach is separated on both sides by streams and barbecue, water and sand - all the same. Behind the second stream at the edge of the bay is a recreation area.

What amenities await tourists on Shamora:

1. In season, alas, long traffic jams and crowding. The parking is large.


2. Sun loungers and parasols for rent.


3. All kinds of watercraft.


4. Snorkeling, diving, sports grounds.


5. Cycling and sailing, trike, mule tours.


6. Water rides and sports, cultural and recreational activities.


7. Annual hangouts of Seaside bikers.


8. Discos, music festivals held in coastal bars.


9. A lot of offers from catering. But, it should be noted that eating should not be anywhere. There are establishments that are very "tasty" and respect customers. There are those where you can easily get poisoning.


When is the best time to go?

The best time to relax on the Far East coast is July and August. At this time, the temperature of water and air reaches maximum levels: about + 20°C on ground and slightly lower in the ocean. Summer in Primorye is rather short and foggy, especially in the southern regions - the humidity there is much higher, which also contributes to a comfortable rest.

Infrastructure

Typical accommodation that is available to travelers coming to Shamora is small guest houses near the beach. These are, as a rule, inexpensive options that are difficult to make any high demands.

Within a few kilometers from the coast or in neighboring bays, guest houses, recreation centers, where there are a few more amenities and civilizations, offer their services. There are places for picnics, playgrounds for kids. For those who like to sleep in a tent and cook on a campfire - an inexpensive guarded campground.


Country Guest House , located relatively close to Lazurnaya Bay, provides all amenities in summer and winter. The guests are available wireless Internet, swimming pool, various sports equipment. The rooms have air conditioning, refrigerator with TV. Everything you need for making tea or coffee is provided. Breakfast is included. If you want to explore the local cuisine, you can find several restaurants nearby.


You can have a wonderful lunch on the beach itself. Here, small barbecue form an entire hospitable street that stretches along the entire coast. Those who have been visiting Shamora for a long time have their favorite places where they usually stop to buy steaming tortilla or hitching, eat aromatic barbecue.


Most cafes are represented by Armenian cuisine. Restaurants serve seafood, Uzbek and Georgian dishes. You can sit in a stylish country restaurant with sea views. Many people who have visited Shamora note simple and tasty food in the cafe "U grachika", there are cozy gazebos and fountains.


You can try savory Korean snacks at the Vladivostok market. In exotic restaurants - fried soup, grilled squid, roe deer, someone will like Japanese dorayaki drenched in maple sauce.
