Vong beach

Vong Beach is probably the first beach that many tourists will see. After all, it is here that the majority of boats, ferries with those who wish to relax on the island of Phu Quoc arrive. The economic industry on the coast is constantly evolving, for which new piers are being built, therefore it is obvious that the beach will undergo more and more changes over time.


Photos

Beach description

Vong Beach territory is located on the southeast coast of Phu Quoc Island, near Duong Dong and the ferry terminal. Therefore, it is often possible to hear, that the beach is considered to be an industrial zone. But a good asphalt road leads to these parts from anywhere in the island, so it is quite easy to get to the beach and from it. It is possible to travel and move around the coast by bus, taxi or rent a bike.


The coastline is covered with sand and trees are quite common. At the same time, water is often contaminated, slightly thick by consistency and contains jellyfish, sea urchins. In general, in the area of the rest area it is sometimes possible to see not only them, since right behind the beach there is a forest, where snakes and lizards live, and in the mountains there are macaques and bats.


The beach is rather narrow, the ocean near the coast is shallow, therefore it is convenient for swimming, but for rest with children it is better to choose a cleaner beach in Vietnam. In addition, numerous ships, people at the berths and industrial activity turn the rest here mostly into observing the stormy life of the island, rather than relaxation and complete relaxing.


In the dry season at noon, it becomes so hot, that vacationers do not want to walk on the hot sand, so more and more tourists on the beaches of the island can be seen in the morning and in the late afternoon. During the day, most of them prefer to rest with a book on the sunbeds or in the cool pools on the hotel territory. But during the rainy season it is quite aggressive here: winds blow and waves rise.


When is it better to go?

Since Vietnam is a country stretching from north to south, the climate in its different parts is slightly different. In the north, high humidity and temperature are observed from May to October, but in the remaining months it is slightly drier and cooler. In the South, the rainy season is longer, lasting from may to November, but even after it ends, the humidity does not subside. Therefore, it is better to go to popular resorts in the south of the country in winter or early spring.

Infrastructure

Since the beach serves as a starting point for every tourist on the island, it is customary to consider it a kind of “transit point” where is a pier, restaurants for feeding, but there are absolutely no accommodation services near the beach. Moreover, the food here is served in fairly good quality: These are traditional Vietnamese dishes or European seafood. At the piers there is also a sale of light snacks and drinks.


If you decide to start the trip from this beach, then you can stay in Bamboo Cottages & Restaurant. These are not good rooms and good service only, but also tasty, wholesome food, and there is also the opportunity for a family relaxation. The hotel offers to its guests boat excursions or kayaking, within which you can go snorkelling or fishing, and in the evening, while listening sound of the sea and a beautiful view of the sunset, it is also allowed to fry the catch.
