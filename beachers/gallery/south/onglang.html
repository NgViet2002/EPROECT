Ong Lang beach

Ong Lang Beach is the exact opposite of the nearest town, Duong Dong, where life is "boiling" literally. Here, the natural element completely captures the whole atmosphere: the sound of waves, rocky terrain, clear water, white sand and wild vegetation. Everything looks as if a person’s hand was not at all attached to this resort. Naturalness, peace and peace are the main terms that characterize Ong Lang Beach.


Photos

Beach description

The beach occupies a leading position in the northwest direction of the island. The location is quite convenient, because the road from the airport takes only half an hour, and it is even possible to get by taxi. A sufficiently long territory (about 10 km) can hardly be called a perfectly flat coastline, since it is rather a series of bays, that have small overlaps of black smooth stones or rocks.


The rocky mass in the area of the beach bring to Ong Lang Beach a very special air - clean and cool. While on the highway it’s still stuffy and dusty, there is a refreshing sea and the shadow of luxurious palm trees. The beach is covered with white fine sand and only occasionally there are stones, that are not at all dangerous for vacationers. The entry into the water is convenient, with a sloping slope and almost without waves.


There are always plenty of tourists on the beach, because the panoramas of these places will not leave anyone indifferent, especially during sunset, when everything is really in harmony. However, it cannot be said, that the beach is crowded or there are too many people on it, no, everything is to acceptable degree. The territory is not only constantly cleaned, but the climate is also inclined to cleanliness on the beach and in the water, because in the dry season, the east wind blows mainly, so the waves go from the coast, taking with them the rest of the garbage.


Literally, the entire territory of the beach is saturated with romance and an amazing lyrical spirit. At sunset, there are always a lot of photographers, who want to capture their best shots. Exactly the same, as during romantic or wedding photo shoots on the coast of the cleanest ocean. Vacationers and local beach staff can simply be envied. Sea, sun, waves, the smell of fresh seafood and exotic fruits - as if there was no better place.


When is it better to go?

Since Vietnam is a country stretching from north to south, the climate in its different parts is slightly different. In the north, high humidity and temperature are observed from May to October, but in the remaining months it is slightly drier and cooler. In the South, the rainy season is longer, lasting from may to November, but even after it ends, the humidity does not subside. Therefore, it is better to go to popular resorts in the south of the country in winter or early spring.

Infrastructure

There, at Ong Lang Beach, is no such developed infrastructure as in neighboring resorts. At the same time, several hotels and guest houses are located along the beach, but they are as were hidden in the depths of the beach. Here, among palm trees and branching trees, narrow paths and cobbled streets, where is everything necessary for relaxation. Grocery stores with local delicacies and souvenir pavilions, cafes and bars, as well as restaurants with a wide range of dishes and different types of cuisine. Nevertheless, the territory of the beach and the surrounding areas are still undergoing changes, because there are more and more people wishing to stay within natural silence every year.


Accommodation here is also very diverse, and if cheaper rooms can be located within ten minutes walk from the beach, then more expensive resorts meet guests right on the territory of Ong Lang Beach. For instance, Camia Resort & Spa, is ready to offer not only excellent service, but also the opportunity to use the jacuzzi, restaurant and children's room.
